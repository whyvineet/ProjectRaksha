Project Raksha

Empowering Women's Safety Through Technology

What is Project Raksha?

Project Raksha is an innovative concept aimed at enhancing women's safety using cutting-edge technology. Our goal is to create a comprehensive system that provides real-time support and empowers women to navigate their environments with confidence.

Key Features

Voice-Activated SOS

Quickly activate emergency alerts using a secret voice command, sending your location to trusted contacts.

Secure Route Planning

Get recommendations for the safest routes, taking into account well-lit areas and safety landmarks.

Real-Time Safety Updates

Stay informed about your surroundings with crowdsourced safety information and alerts.

Community Support

Connect with nearby users in case of emergencies, creating a network of mutual support.

How It Works

Project Raksha uses advanced algorithms to process various data points, including:

  • User-reported safety information
  • Location data of safety landmarks
  • Real-time traffic and lighting conditions
  • Historical safety patterns

This data is analyzed to provide you with the most up-to-date safety recommendations and support.
